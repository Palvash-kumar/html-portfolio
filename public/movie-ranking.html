The Best Movies According to Palvash

My top 3 movies of all time.

1. The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Starring Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman, this movie is a timeless classic that never fails to move me to tears. The story of hope and redemption is one that I return to again and again.

2. The Dark Knight (2008)

Christopher Nolan's masterpiece is a thrilling ride that keeps me on the edge of my seat. The performances of the cast, particularly Heath Ledger's iconic portrayal of the Joker, are simply unforgettable.
